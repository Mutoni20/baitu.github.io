Baitu Group Limited is a leading brand marketing, events management and media communication conglomate in East Africa community with its head office in Uganda

OUR CEO

Honorable Balaam Barugahara Ateenyi commonly known as Balaam is a Ugandan Politician, business man, music and events promoter. He is the minister of state for youth and children affairs in Uganda. Balaam is the proprietor and owner of: Original Best Water company based in South Sudan, Radio One in South Sudan, the first privately owned media house he started in 2009. He is also the proprietor and Managing Director of Radio4 (Uganda), Radio 7 (Masindi), Radio8 (Masaka) and Radio8 (Elgon region) and the MTN Uganda distributor for Kigezi sub region. Balaam is a Real Estate Developer with hotels in Kampala and South Sudan, including a 5 start Hotel in Masindi, which was commissioned by President Yoweri Museveni in September 2022.
