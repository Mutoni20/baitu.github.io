DID YOU KNOW?

Baitu Group Limited started as Balaam Enterprises in 1997 and was later incorporated as Balaam Marketing Agency in 2011. Over the years, with dedication, resilience, competence and forthrightness in our dealings backed with visionary leadership and management team, we have grown and diversified our portfolios cutting across; brand development, marketing promotions, events management, media services, Innovations & Designs, rental service, trading and Logistics.


With over 25 years in the industry, Baitu Group Ltd remains the first among others in provision of innovative strategies and solutions to our numerous clients and partners satisfactorily.
